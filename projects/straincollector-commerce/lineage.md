Here is every unique parent used in your breeding lines, along with their known histories.

1. Grape Ape

Lineage: Afghani x Skunk #1 (select phenotype)

History/Breeder: A classic indica-dominant strain bred by Apothecary Genetics (associated with Barney's Farm). It's known for its potent grape and berry flavors and deep purple coloration. It was a popular commercial strain in the late 1990s and 2000s.

2. Indiana Bubble Gum

Lineage: Original Bubble Gum (Serious Seeds) / (likely an Indiana cut)

History/Breeder: This refers to a specific, prized clone-only phenotype of the original Bubble Gum strain that was circulated in Indiana, USA. The original Bubble Gum from Serious Seeds is a hybrid of an Indiana sativa and a Skunk. The "Indiana Bubble Gum" cut is renowned for its powerful, sweet, and exactly bubble gum-like flavor.



4. Chem. Sister (Chem Sister)

Lineage: Sibling to Chemdog '91 from the same bag of seeds.

History/Breeder: From the original bag of Chemdog seeds (purchased at a Grateful Dead show in 1991), several keeper phenotypes were found: Chemdog 91, Chemdog B, and Chem Sister. She is a direct sibling to the legendary Chemdog 91, sharing its gassy, diesel, and skunky profile but with her own unique expression.




7. Purest Indica

Lineage: A collective term for landrace Afghani/Pakistani genetics.

History/Breeder: steve murphy purest indica, one of the earliest pure Afghan plants, mother to norhtern lights.

8. Chem 91 (Chemdog '91)

Lineage: From the original 1991 Chemdog bag seed.

History/Breeder: The most famous phenotype from the original Chemdog bag. It is one of the most influential strains of all time, founding the "Chem" family and contributing to the Diesel lineage. It's a clone-only strain revered for its pungent fuel, chemical, and skunk aroma with a devastating high.

9. Cheetah Piss

Lineage: Gelato 41 x Lemonade

History/Breeder: A modern hybrid from the breeders at Sherbinski. Despite the name, it's known for a complex aroma of lemon, citrus, and fuel with a creamy, almost urinous (in a good way) undertone. It won 1st Place at the 2017 Michigan High Times Cannabis Cup.

10. NL2 (Northern Lights #2)

Lineage: Original Afghani landrace.

History/Breeder: Northern Lights is one of the most famous indica strains ever, originally bred in Seattle. NL #2 is a specific, pure Afghani phenotype from the original line, known for its rapid flowering, dense resinous buds, and classic spicy-hash aroma. It's a foundational building block for countless hybrids.

11. Cookies n Chem

Lineage: Girl Scout Cookies x Chemdog

History/Breeder: A cross made by breeders to combine two modern legends. It brings together the sweet, earthy, and slightly minty flavor of GSC with the raw power and fuel of Chemdog.

12. 97 Super Sour

Lineage: Massive Creations 1997 sierra sour x f4 818 headband 

13. Destroyer

Lineage: Meao Thai sativa x (Mexican/Colombian)

History/Breeder: A legendary sativa line bred by Cannabiogen/Ace Seeds. It is a multi-year project to stabilize a hybrid of a killer Meao Thai (Thailand) landrace and other high-quality sativas. It is famous for its extremely potent, clear, and psychedelic high and complex hazey/woody/citrus terpene profile.

14. SSH (Super Silver Haze)

Lineage: (Skunk x Haze) x (NL x Haze)

History/Breeder: A classic Haze hybrid from Mr. Nice Seedbank (born from the original Sensi Seeds stock). A multiple Cannabis Cup winner, it combines the yield and structure of Skunk and Northern Lights with the soaring, cerebral high and spicy-citrus flavor of the original Haze.



17. Ohaze (Original Haze)

Lineage: A hybrid of landraces from Colombia, Mexico, Thailand, and India.

History/Breeder: The legendary Haze brothers (R. Haze, S. Haze) in California created this sativa masterpiece in the 1960s and 70s. It's a pure, long-flowering sativa known for its soaring, energetic, and creative high and a complex spicy, sweet, and citrus aroma. It is the foundation for all "Haze" hybrids.



19. Chrystal

Lineage: White Widow x Northern Lights

History/Breeder: A strain from the breeder Mr. Nice, created by Shantibaba. It combines two of the most resinous and potent indicas of its time to create a frosty, reliable, and powerful plant.

22. G13

Lineage: Purported to be a government-grown Afghani indica.

History/Breeder: One of cannabis's great legends. The story is that this clone was smuggled out of a U.S. government research facility. While the story is likely apocryphal, the "G13" cut that circulated is a supremely potent, heavy-yielding indica with a narcotic effect. It was used by many breeders in the 2000s.

23. Double Widow

Lineage: Likely a cross or backcross of White Widow.

History/Breeder: This name typically refers to a specific, potent cross or an IBL of White Widow (a Brazilian sativa landrace x South Indian hybrid). It implies a doubling down on the Widow's potency and resin production.

24. Shiva

Lineage: Northern Lights #1 x Skunk #1

History/Breeder: A classic strain from Sensi Seeds. It combines the two most popular base genetics of the 1980s, creating a reliable, fast-flowering, and potent hybrid with a skunky, spicy aroma.

25. Daywrecker (Daywrecker Diesel)

Lineage: Original Diesel (aka Diesel #1) clone-only.

History/Breeder: A specific, elite cut of the Original Diesel (itself a Chemdog sibling) that was famously grown in upstate New York. It is known for its face-melting potency and intense sour fuel aroma. It is a cornerstone of the East Coast sour diesel lineage.

26. G13 x2

Lineage: Likely a cross or selfing of the original G13 clone.

History/Breeder: This designation suggests a specific cross where G13 is used on both sides, perhaps a G13 hybrid crossed to another G13 hybrid, or a selfed (S1) version to preserve its traits.

27. Herijuana

Lineage: Petrolia Headstash x Killer New Haven

History/Breeder: Bred by Woodhorse Seeds, Herijuana is a legendary medicinal strain. It is infamous for its extremely high THC content and powerfully sedative, almost narcotic effect. It's a classic "couch-lock" indica used for pain relief and insomnia.

28. Destroyer Pearl

Lineage: A specific, pearl-like phenotype of Destroyer.

History/Breeder: This refers to a specific, highly prized phenotype of the Destroyer line, selected by you or your associates for its exceptional resin production and sativa qualities.

Lineage: athens haze, a specific cut of silver haze.
