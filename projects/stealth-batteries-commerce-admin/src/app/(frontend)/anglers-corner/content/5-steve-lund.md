Steve Lund: A Legacy of Excellence, Anchored in Arizona—

Steve Lund stands as a pillar of the Arizona bass fishing community, renowned for his decades-long commitment to the sport and his impressive record on the water. Since joining the Yuma Bassmasters in 1999, Steve has set the standard for competitive angling in the Southwest, winning the very first state qualifier he entered and quickly establishing himself as a force to be reckoned with. His relentless pursuit of excellence earned him Angler of the Year honors for three consecutive years (2007, 2008, and 2009) with the Yuma club, before he brought his talents to the Surprise Bass Club in 2010.

Steve’s tournament résumé is a testament to his skill, adaptability, and unwavering dedication. He has consistently finished at the top in both state and regional events, including a standout performance at the TNT Fireworks B.A.S.S. Nation Western Regional, where he led the field on Day 1 and secured a top finish. Steve’s ability to read the water, adjust to changing conditions, and outthink the competition has made him a respected mentor and competitor throughout the West.

What truly sets Steve apart is his passion for sharing the sport with others—whether mentoring new anglers, competing alongside his son Kalib (himself a rising pro), or representing Arizona on the national stage. Steve’s approach combines decades of experience, a deep respect for the outdoors, and a commitment to sportsmanship that inspires everyone around him.

With a legacy built on hard work, integrity, and a love for fishing, Steve Lund continues to shape the future of bass angling in Arizona and beyond.
