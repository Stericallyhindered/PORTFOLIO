Kalib Lund: Driven by Dedication, Defined by Success—

Meet Kalib Lund, one of Arizona’s premier professional anglers and a rising star on the national bass fishing scene. Hailing from Yuma, Arizona, Kalib’s passion for fishing was forged in the desert lakes of the Southwest and honed through years of dedication, discipline, and a relentless drive to succeed. As a proud member of the U.S. Marine Corps, Kalib brings the same commitment and focus to the water that he did in service to his country—making him a formidable competitor and a respected figure among his peers.

Kalib’s tournament record speaks for itself: he is a three-time Arizona B.A.S.S. Nation Angler of the Year and a multiple-time tournament champion, including a recent victory at the WWCT Championship alongside partner Zach Hayes. His consistent top finishes and ability to adapt to new waters have earned him a reputation as one of the most versatile and determined anglers in the region. Kalib’s journey has taken him from local club events with the Surprise Bass Club to the national stage, where he continues to chase his dream of qualifying for the Bassmaster Classic.

What sets Kalib apart is not just his skill with a rod and reel, but his commitment to preparation, sportsmanship, and sharing his love of fishing with others. Sponsored by industry leaders like Toyota, Bass Pro Shops, and more, Kalib trusts only the best gear to power his pursuit of big bass and bigger dreams. Whether he’s competing in the heart of Arizona or exploring new fisheries across the country, Kalib Lund is always ready to inspire, compete, and create unforgettable moments on the water.
